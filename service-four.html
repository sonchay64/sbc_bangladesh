Skills Training & Capacity Building

  • Skills Training & Capacity Building

We conduct training programs to equip professionals with the skills to enhance their capabilities, drive business growth, implement sustainable practices, and excel in a competitive digital landscape.

We offer certification training to equip professionals with essential skills for growth, efficiency, and excellence, as follows:

Sustainability Leadership Training Program (SLTP)

This program equips leaders with skills to integrate sustainability into business strategies, focusing on ESG leadership, stakeholder engagement, and developing actionable sustainability roadmaps for long-term success.

Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) Training

This training helps businesses align with global reporting standards like GRI, IFRS/ISSB, and SASB, enhancing transparency, tracking ESG performance, and ensuring regulatory compliance in sustainability reporting.

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) Professional Training Program

This program trains participants to assess and manage environmental impacts, covering EIA principles, impact analysis, mitigation strategies, and ensuring regulatory compliance in project planning.

Six Sigma Training

This training teaches the DMAIC methodology (Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, and Control) to improve process efficiency, reduce defects, and drive continuous improvement and operational excellence in business operations.

Professional Digital Marketing and E-commerce Certification Training

This program covers digital marketing strategies, including SEO, social media, PPC, and e-commerce management, enabling professionals to drive online sales, boost brand visibility, and enhance engagement.

Professional Certification Training on Data Analytics

This course covers Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI, SPSS, and AMOS for data analytics. Participants will learn data manipulation, statistical analysis, visualization, and predictive modeling. Key topics include database querying, business intelligence, hypothesis testing, and Structural Equation Modeling (SEM), equipping learners with practical skills for data-driven decision-making.

Soft Business Skills Training

This training develops essential interpersonal skills such as leadership, emotional intelligence, negotiation, conflict resolution, and time management, preparing professionals to excel in collaborative business environments and build stronger relationships.
