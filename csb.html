The Center for Sustainable Business (CSB), established under SBC Bangladesh, is committed to advancing sustainability and responsible business practices. With a strong focus on Capacity Building & Promotion, Research & Innovation, and Policy Advocacy, CSBP serves as a dynamic hub for knowledge exchange, research, and collaboration in sustainable business development. By fostering partnerships and driving actionable insights, CSBP empowers businesses and policymakers to create lasting positive impacts.

Situated in Mymensingh Division, the centre works closely with Universities, NGOs, Government Bodies, and SMEs to foster impactful strategies that drive long-term economic, social, and environmental progress.

The primary aim of CSBP is to bridge the gap between sustainability theory and practice by equipping businesses with actionable strategies, evidence-based research, and policy recommendations. The centre seeks to:

  • Promote sustainable and responsible business practices in key industries such as agribusiness, fisheries, and SMEs, ensuring economic, social, and environmental resilience for long-term growth.

  • Empower women and youth entrepreneurs through training, mentorship, social business development, and green innovation, fostering sustainable growth and economic inclusion.

  • Conduct cutting-edge research on sustainability strategy, sustainable business models, sustainability performance, and ESG assessment, providing data-driven insights for industry transformation.

  • Advocate for policies that integrate sustainability into regional development, strengthening responsible business practices and long-term economic resilience.

Your trusted partner in ESG and Digital Marketing solutions. We help businesses integrate sustainability into their core strategies while leveraging digital transformation for growth.
